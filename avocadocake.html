Avocado cake


I made this recipe inspired by Juli Rochinas. First I saw the picture, without knowing what the cake had, and when I saw the recipe I really loved it because I did not expect the main ingredient was avocado, and the ingredients were so few â€¦


We can find avocados during all seasons -and I love to change the butter for avocado-



For the base:


5 Dates.

1 Cup of toasted almonds.

1 Cup of shredded coconut.



For the filling:


1 Big avocado (Malaga origin).

1 Glass of a soft oil (I put sunflower, it could be coconut).

1 Coconut yogurt 100% natural.

Juice of 1 lime and 2 lemons (the original recipe also had the skin and only the juice of the limes, but to me it seems smoother this way).

Agave syrup (at your discretion, I do not like stuff so sweet).
